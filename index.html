Sanicon Valley

THE INTERPLANETARY FUTURE CITY

Welcome to Sanicon Valley, the one and only interplanetary future city, that lies on the great city of San Jose, far more advanced than many other cities in the world. Our city blends advanced technology, with nature, and repurposing waste for the greater good.

Sanicon Valley is home to more than 1,000,000 residents, as it is the hub of social, economic, and technological advancement.

Our city uses advanced technology to repurpose all kinds of waste to useful nutrients, energy, fuel, and more. We keep our city as simple as possible as we fix the major problem which has never been solved for decades in San Jose, which is to improve our farm to table system.

Water & Vertical Farming

A major problem is the extreme water shortage in San Jose. We will repurpose shower water which can be used to water the plants. Every house has a vertical farm in their backyard, ensuring everyone has a private grocery store.

The Community App

Everyone will have an app to order a robot for fresh produce. Surplus fruits/vegetables trigger community notifications, ensuring no food is wasted and even the poor are provided for. This builds a strong community which San Jose currently lacks.

Interplanetary Solutions

Our city has little to no carbon footprint. We trap CO2 for fizz drinks, dry ice, and oxygen conversion. We use food waste as compost and cooking oil for soaps. Remaining waste becomes biogas for cars.

Zoning & Space

There will be no supermarkets for produce, only meat, snacks, and drinks. This creates more space for recreational areas. Since every backyard is a farm, we have more room for industrial areas, schools, and parks.

Plastic & Innovation

We still use plastic, but with a twist: bins containing plastic-eating bacteria. This allows for immediate decomposition, meaning practically zero plastic waste in our environment.

Nuclear Power

Our main energy source is Nuclear Energy. Only two plants power the entire Sanicon Valley, providing the massive energy required for our AI-driven infrastructure.

The Robotic Home

Every house has a backyard bot. An optional household bot acts as a "simulated visitor," allowing you to communicate and share produce with others instantly, without even needing a phone.

Socialization & Layout

Offices are arranged in matrices around central squares filled with restaurants. This encourages business owners and citizens to combine ideas, fostering the ultimate collaborative environment.
