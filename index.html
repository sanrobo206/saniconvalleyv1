I. THE HUB

Welcome to Sanicon Valley, the one and only interplanetary future city. Our city blends advanced technology with nature, repurposing waste for the greater good. It is the hub of social, economic, and technological advancement.

We solve the decades-old problems of San Jose through an elite farm to table system and 100% waste reclamation protocol.

II. BIO-SYSTEMS

Sanicon Valley solves the extreme water shortage by repurposing shower water to sustain vertical backyard farms. This ensures every citizen has a private grocery store in their own backyard.

Surplus crops are shared via a community app and robotic couriers, building social bonds that San Jose currently lacks.

III. NUCLEAR CORE

One interplanetary solution which makes this city unique is our nuclear energy grid. Just two plants power the entire valley, providing the main energy source for AI-driven infrastructure.

We trap carbon dioxide for fizz drinks and oxygen conversion, leaving a zero-carbon footprint across the interplanetary grid.

IV. URBAN LOGIC

Every home features a backyard bot for farming and assistance. Our strict zoning laws cap new homes at 3,000 sq ft to maximize industrial space and energy plants.

Offices are arranged in matrices around social squares, spontaneous communication, and collaborative innovation. This is the blueprint for the interplanetary future.
