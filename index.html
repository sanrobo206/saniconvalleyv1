Sanicon Valley

The Interplanetary Future City

Welcome to Sanicon Valley, the one and only interplanetary future city. Lies on the great city of San Jose, far more advanced than many other cities. Blending advanced technology with nature and repurposing waste for the greater good.

Water Solution

Repurposing shower water to keep plants healthy. Every house features a vertical farm in the backyard, ensuring a private grocery store for every citizen.

Robot Distribution

Order fresh produce via a community app and robot delivery. Surplus notifications ensure zero food waste and build the community San Jose currently lacks.

Zero Net Waste

We trap CO2 for fizz drinks and oxygen conversion. Food waste becomes compost; cooking oil becomes soap. Plastic-eating bacteria eliminate pollution instantly.

Nuclear Energy

Our interplanetary solution. Only two plants power the entire valley, fueling the heavy AI infrastructure required for a futuristic society.

Zoning Laws

Strict caps on house sizes (max 3,000 sq ft) to save space for energy plants and schools. Manual labor is fully automated; humans focus on innovation.

Social Matrices

Offices arranged around central squares and restaurants. Combined ideas lead to interplanetary advancement. This is Sanicon Valley.
