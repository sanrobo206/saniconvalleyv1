SANICON VALLEY

THE FUTURE OF SAN JOSE

I. THE MASTERPLAN

Welcome to Sanicon Valley, the one and only interplanetary future city, that lies on the great city of San Jose, far more advanced than many other cities in the world. Our city blends advanced technology, with nature, and repurposing waste for the greater good.

Sanicon Valley is home to more than 1,000,000 residents, as it is the hub of social, economic, and technological advancement, with the forward thinking system of urban planning, and repurposing waste. Our city uses advanced technology to repurpose all kinds of waste to useful nutrients, energy, fuel, and more.

II. BIO-RECLAMATION

There are many problems in San Jose. A major problem is the extreme water shortage. Sanicon Valley not only fixes this problem, but also uses this problem to fix another major problem: keeping plants healthy. We will repurpose shower water which can be used to water the plants.

Also, every house has a vertical farm in their backyard. Everyone will have an app to order a robot for agriculture. Surplus crops notify the community, ensuring no food is wasted and building a strong social bond San Jose currently lacks.

III. NUCLEAR CORE

Our city has little to no carbon footprint. We trap carbon dioxide for various purposes, from fizz drinks to oxygen conversion. One energy solution which makes this city interplanetary is nuclear energy.

Only two plants power the entire Sanicon Valley, providing the main source of energy for AI-driven infrastructure. We complement this with solar, hydro, and wind power for a 100% clean grid.

IV. ROBOTICS & ZONING

Every home features a backyard bot for farming and assistance. Strict zoning laws cap new homes at 3,000 sq ft to prioritize major infrastructure. Hospitals are built for scale, covering 100,000 sq ft across 10 floors to handle the whole valley at once.

Offices are arranged around social squares filled with restaurants and engaging activities. This enables spontaneous communication and collaborative innovation—the blueprint for the interplanetary future.
