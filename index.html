SANICON VALLEY

THE INTERPLANETARY FUTURE CITY

I. THE MASTERPLAN

Welcome to Sanicon Valley, the one and only interplanetary future city, that lies on the great city of San Jose, far more advanced than many other cities in the world. Our city blends advanced technology, with nature, and repurposing waste for the greater good.

Sanicon Valley is home to more than 1,000,000 residents, as it is the hub of social, economic, and technological advancement, with the forward thinking system of urban planning, and repurposing waste. We fix the major problems which have never been solved for decades in San Jose, starting with a revolutionary farm-to-table system.

II. BIO-RECLAMATION

A major problem is the extreme water shortage in San Jose. Sanicon Valley not only fixes this problem, but also uses it to keep plants healthy. We will repurpose shower water which can be used to water the plants. Also, every house has a vertical farm in their backyard.

Everyone will have an app in which they can order a robot which will give the amount and kind of fruit or vegetable upon order. This ensures no food is wasted and builds a strong community, which San Jose currently lacks.

III. NUCLEAR ENERGY GRID

Our city has little to no carbon footprint. We trap carbon dioxide for various different purposes, from fizz drinks to oxygen conversion. One energy solution which makes this city interplanetary is nuclear energy. Two plants are enough to power the entire Sanicon Valley.

This will be the main source of energy, as AI will be heavily used and it needs a lot of energy. We complement this with solar energy for houses and electric vehicle charging stations, and wind turbines in high-velocity areas.

IV. RESIDENTIAL ROBOTICS

Every house will have one robot for the backyard. Another robot is optional and will be for helping with household tasks. Just by sitting, you can use the bot to instantly talk to anyone you want. It is like having a simulated visitor.

This city has strict zoning laws. New houses have a maximum area of 3,000 square feet excluding the backyard. Hospitals will be massive, with a maximum area of 100,000 square feet. Just 5 of these hospitals is enough to handle the whole Sanicon Valley at once.

V. SOCIAL COMMERCE

Offices will be placed in a perfect way for socialization. They will be close by so people can roam and combine ideas. These companies will be placed such that it is on the outline of a rectangle meaning the center is a square where there are many restaurants and engaging activities.

With all of this, Sanicon Valley is the hub of technological innovation and a global blueprint for the future.
