SANICON VALLEY

SAN JOSE REIMAGINED

I. HUB OF ADVANCEMENT

Welcome to Sanicon Valley, the one and only interplanetary future city. Our city blends advanced technology with nature, repurposing waste for the greater good. It is the hub of social, economic, and technological advancement.

We fix the decadal problems of San Jose by implementing a revolutionary farm-to-table system and repurposing every ounce of waste into useful nutrients and energy.

II. NUCLEAR POWER GRID

Sanicon Valley operates on nuclear energy. Just two plants power the entire valley, providing the massive energy loads required for interplanetary AI-driven infrastructure.

We use every possible method: solar energy, hydroelectric power, and high-velocity wind turbines to ensure a perfect zero-carbon footprint across the grid.

III. THE ROBOTIC BACKYARD

Every house features a vertical farm in their backyard. We repurpose shower water to sustain growth. Integrated robotic systems distribute food surpluses to the community, building social bonds that San Jose currently lacks.

Our zoning laws are strict: new houses are capped at 3,000 square feet to prioritize industrial space and social engage centers for commerce and innovation.
